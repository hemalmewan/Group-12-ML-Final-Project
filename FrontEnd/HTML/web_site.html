  • DATASET OVERVIEW

    Dataset Summary

    This dataset contains 1,938 furniture delivery records across 14 attributes covering product details, pricing, shipping, ratings, and delivery outcomes.

    Target Variable

    Delivery status originally had six outcome labels. These were simplified into three key categories: Delivered, Failed Delivery, and Ongoing.

    Purpose

    The dataset enables us to analyze delivery patterns and develop predictive models that support more reliable and efficient logistics decision-making.

  • ANALYSIS & INSIGHTS
    Delivery Performance Overview

    This chart shows the overall proportion of orders classified as Delivered, Failed Delivery, and Ongoing. It helps identify the dominant outcome and any performance concerns in the fulfillment process.

    Delivery Performance Across Product Categories

    This chart compares delivery outcomes across furniture categories, helping identify which product types are delivered reliably and which categories may encounter delays or failed deliveries.

    Brand Performance in Delivery Outcomes

    This visualization compares delivery success across different furniture brands. It helps identify reliable brands with consistent delivery performance and those that may require logistic or supply improvements.

    Pricing and Category Impact on Delivery Outcomes

    This visualization highlights how product cost and category together influence delivery success. It helps reveal whether certain price ranges or furniture types are more prone to delays, rescheduling, or failed deliveries.
