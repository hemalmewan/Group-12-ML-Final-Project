    Dataset Summary

    This dataset contains 1,938 furniture delivery records across 14 attributes covering product details, pricing, shipping, ratings, and delivery outcomes.

    Target Variable

    Delivery status originally had six outcome labels. These were simplified into three key categories: Delivered, Failed Delivery, and Ongoing.

    Purpose

    The dataset enables us to analyze delivery patterns and develop predictive models that support more reliable and efficient logistics decision-making.
